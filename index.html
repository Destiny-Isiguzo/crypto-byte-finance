Frequently Asked Questions

Explore our Frequently Asked Questions to find answers to queries about our investment and trading platform. If you have more questions, hit the live chat button to start a chat with a customer care agent.

How do I create an account

To create an account on our platform, simply click on the "Get started" button on our homepage and fill out the required information. You will then receive an email to verify your account, after which you can log in and start trading/investing.

What cryptocurrencies do you support

We currently support Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Litecoin (LTC), Bitcoin Cash (BCH), and many more cryptocurrencies being added.

How do I deposit funds into my account

You can deposit funds using a credit/debit card, bank transfer, or by transferring cryptocurrencies from an external wallet. Simply navigate to the "Deposit" section of your account dashboard and follow the instructions.

Is my personal information and account secure

Absolutely! We prioritize the safety and security of our users' information and accounts. Our platform uses state-of-the-art encryption and security measures to protect your data and ensure that all transactions are secure.
